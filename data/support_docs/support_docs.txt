Payment failures may occur due to expired credit cards.
Customers should verify the card expiry date.

Payment failures may occur because the issuing bank rejected the transaction.
Customers should contact their bank.

Payment failures may occur because fraud detection systems blocked the transaction.
Customers should verify their identity.

Login issues may occur because users forgot their password.
Users should reset their password.

Subscription issues may occur because payment renewal failed.
Customers should update billing information.
